Our Mission

We aim to empower citizens across India to take cleanliness into their own hands. Our mission is to spread awareness, organize clean-up drives, and build a nation we’re proud to live in.

Why Join Us?

  • Be part of a government-recognized NGO
  • Exclusive access to cleanliness drive events
  • Make a real impact in your city
